🏢 General
What is Acentrik Technology Solutions LLC?
+
Acentrik Technology Solutions LLC is a global IT consulting services company based in Irving, TX. We deliver advisory and professional managed services to enterprises and clients worldwide who are looking for value, flexibility, and excellent service. We are a digital transformation partner focused on providing cost-effective, integrated, responsive, and trustworthy IT solutions.

+
Our offices are located at 4425 W Airport Fwy, Suite 207, Irving, TX 75062. You can reach us by phone at +1 (469) 670-4364 or by email at hr@acentriktech.com. While we're based in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, we serve clients globally.
What industries do you serve?
+
We serve enterprises across a wide range of industries including financial services, retail, healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, and technology companies. Our techno-functional experts bring industry-specific knowledge along with deep technology expertise to every engagement.
Do you work with small businesses or only large enterprises?
+
We work with organizations of all sizes — from growing small and medium businesses to large enterprises. Our flexible service model allows us to tailor our engagement to the scale and budget of your organization while delivering the same high-quality expertise.

What core services does Acentrik offer?

Our core services include: Product Development, Application Development & Support, Intelligent Operations (AI/ML/Data Analytics), DevOps Engineering, Test Automation, and Help Desk Services. We also offer IT Consulting and Application Modernization services for enterprise clients.

Absolutely. Application Modernization is one of our key offerings. We help enterprise organizations transform legacy processes into flexible, modular application development portfolios by implementing next-generation processes defined by speed, efficiency, and resilience. We use an agile approach to enhance digital transformation while keeping pace with competition.
Do you provide ongoing support after project delivery?

Yes. Our Application Development & Support service includes ongoing post-deployment support. We also offer dedicated Help Desk services for unparalleled 24/7 support, ensuring your operations remain smooth and uninterrupted after go-live.
⚙️ Our Process
What does the engagement process look like?
+
Our process starts with a Discovery phase where we assess your challenges, objectives, and technology landscape. We then move to Strategy (designing the right solution architecture), Execution (implementing with best practices and full transparency), and finally ongoing Support and continuous improvement post-deployment.
How do you ensure project quality?
+
We follow best industry practices and implement comprehensive Test Automation solutions to ensure quality and reliability. Our teams use continuous testing integration, regression testing, and performance testing throughout the development lifecycle to catch issues early and accelerate time-to-market.
Do you follow Agile methodologies?
+
Yes. We adopt an agile approach to application development to enhance digital transformation efficiently while keeping pace with the competition. Agile enables us to deliver iterative value, respond to changing requirements, and maintain positive customer experiences throughout the engagement.
